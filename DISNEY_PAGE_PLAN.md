# Disney World Informational Landing Page - Planning Document

## Goal
Create a comprehensive, informative landing page about Disney World that serves as a helpful resource for visitors planning their trip.

## Target Audience
- First-time visitors planning their trip
- Returning visitors looking for updated information
- People researching Disney World before booking

## Key Information to Present

### 1. Overview/Introduction
- What is Disney World?
- Brief history and significance
- Why visit?
- Location and basic info

### 2. The Four Theme Parks
**Magic Kingdom**
- Iconic Cinderella Castle
- Classic Disney attractions
- Best for: Families with young children
- Must-see attractions list
- Tips for visiting

**EPCOT**
- Future World and World Showcase
- Cultural experiences
- Best for: Adults, food lovers, culture enthusiasts
- Must-see attractions
- Festival information

**Hollywood Studios**
- Movie and TV themed
- Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge
- Best for: Movie fans, thrill seekers
- Must-see attractions
- Tips for visiting

**Animal Kingdom**
- Nature and wildlife
- Pandora - The World of Avatar
- Best for: Nature lovers, adventure seekers
- Must-see attractions
- Conservation message

### 3. Planning Your Visit
- Best times to visit
- How many days needed
- Park hopper vs single park tickets
- Genie+ and Lightning Lane explained
- FastPass+ (if still relevant)
- Dining reservations
- Where to stay (on-site vs off-site)

### 4. Tips & Tricks
- Arrive early (rope drop)
- Use mobile app
- Plan your route
- Take breaks
- Stay hydrated
- Use rider swap for families
- Best photo spots
- Hidden secrets

### 5. Dining & Food
- Quick service vs table service
- Character dining
- Must-try snacks
- Best restaurants by park
- Dietary restrictions

### 6. Transportation
- Getting to Disney World
- Transportation within property (monorail, buses, boats, Skyliner)
- Parking information

### 7. Special Events & Seasons
- Holidays at Disney
- Special events calendar
- Seasonal decorations
- Festival seasons

### 8. Wait Times (Future - API Integration)
- Real-time wait times
- Best times to ride popular attractions
- Historical wait time data

## UI Components to Consider (Tailwind CSS)

### Navigation
- Sticky header with smooth scroll
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Clear section links

### Hero Section
- Large, impactful hero image or video background
- Clear headline and value proposition
- Call-to-action button

### Cards/Grids
- Park cards with images
- Information cards for tips
- Feature cards for key information
- Hover effects and interactions

### Sections
- Alternating background colors
- Clear typography hierarchy
- Spacious layouts
- Visual breaks between sections

### Interactive Elements
- Accordion/FAQ for common questions
- Tabs for different information categories
- Image galleries/carousels
- Hover states on cards

### Additional Elements
- Search functionality (optional, if needed)

### Footer
- Links to resources
- Social media
- Additional information
- Disclaimer about unofficial site

## Design Principles

### Color Scheme
- Disney blue (#003d82) as primary
- White and light grays for backgrounds
- Accent colors for different parks
- High contrast for accessibility

### Typography
- Clean, readable fonts
- Clear hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Good line spacing
- Readable font sizes

### Layout
- Minimal, clean design
- Plenty of white space
- Grid-based layouts
- Responsive design (mobile-first)

### Imagery
- High-quality photos of parks
- Consistent image styling
- Alt text for accessibility
- Optimized file sizes

## Content Structure

1. **Hero** - Welcome message, main CTA
2. **Introduction** - What is Disney World?
3. **Parks Overview** - Grid of 4 parks with key info
4. **Detailed Park Sections** - Expandable or separate sections for each park
5. **Planning Guide** - Tips, tickets, Genie+, etc.
6. **Dining Guide** - Food and restaurant information
7. **Tips & Tricks** - Helpful visitor tips
8. **Special Events** - Calendar and seasonal info
9. **Wait Times** - (Placeholder for future API integration)
10. **Resources** - Additional helpful links and information

## Tailwind UI Components to Use

- **Cards** - For parks, tips, information blocks
- **Grids** - For organizing content
- **Buttons** - CTAs and navigation
- **Accordions** - For FAQs or expandable content
- **Badges** - For tags, categories
- **Dividers** - Visual separation
- **Icons** - For visual interest (Heroicons or similar)

## Success Metrics (For Planning)

- Clear, scannable information
- Easy navigation
- Helpful for trip planning
- Visually appealing
- Accessible to all users
- Fast loading
- Mobile-friendly
